Predicted Challenges in Learning Blind Typing

1. Inconsistent Participation

Maintaining regular practice sessions can be difficult when progress seems slow.

2. Environmental Distractions

Background noise and interruptions break concentration needed for learning.

3. Fatigue

Both mental and physical tiredness from learning new finger movements.

4. Physical Discomfort

Poor posture or hand position can lead to strain or injury over time.

5. Loss of Motivation

Initial typing speed often decreases before improving, which can be discouraging.
